Announcements!

Hello Everyone! I hope everyone had a fruitful April Fools and Easter! With APR drill this week there are a few things to go over and I’m sure you will have questions regarding current events. First of all, we will no longer performing flight ops as all funding has halted. Other questions will be either answered or at minimum discussed during this drill period. The new Gate code is 698412. For now these are the main points of focus for this drill:

Drill Agenda

  • Shadow Divestment:

    By now everyone should’ve already been sent the EXORD regarding our system. Full time staff are currently working on sanitizing equipment for turn in. Tasks will stem from the need of finishing certain tasks by deadlines mentioned on the EXORD.

  • Admin:

    Please be flexible during the weekend if any personnel pull you aside to fill out paperwork. There will be an iPERMS records review for certain personnel. Be prepared to sit down and review your records for accuracy.

  • ACFT Makeup:

    For personnel who SUTA’d the prior drill, you will be completing a ACFT this weekend. Please bring a pair of PT’s (or equivalent if you don’t have any issued).

  • Driver’s Training

    We will continue with training and driver’s exams this week to get everyone licensed. Note there may be an opportunity for a Master Driver to come down in May as well.

  • AWT/Safety Classes:

    In lieu of flight ops, we will continue AWT/Safety classes to meet our requirements for the year. Personnel will be assigned to classes.

  • Chow:

    Due to S4 requirements, we are grabbing mermites from the field again. Due to this, personnel will possibly have to go out on the economy for B/L/D on Wed and Thurs. This is due to BN only drilling on Fri – Sun. RTI at the writing of this drill letter has not confirmed us for Wed and Thurs.
